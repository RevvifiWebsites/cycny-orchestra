Audition Information

The audition process for the Youth Orchestra, CYCNY, is designed to identify young musicians with passion and potential. Applicants will perform a prepared solo piece showcasing their technical and musical abilities, demonstrate proficiency in scales, and complete a brief sight-reading exercise. Auditions are friendly and supportive, providing an opportunity for candidates to shine while sharing their love of music.
