Introduction

The Youth Orchestra is a Queens-based Youth Orchestra founded in 1996. The orchestra holds its open audition in the fall for members; it meets for rehearsal on each Saturday from 1-4 PM at MS 158 in Bayside, Queens, NYC from October to June. Our outstanding faculty members are; Chijen Christopher Chung, director, conductor & string; Chi-Ching Grace Lin, percussion; Chiwei Chang, wind & librarian; Lovell Chang, wind & brass; Jeffrey Liang, music consultant, Patsy Chen, artistic director and Jason Chang, executive director.
